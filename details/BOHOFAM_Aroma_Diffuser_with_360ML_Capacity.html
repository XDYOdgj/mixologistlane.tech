BOHOFAM Aroma Essential Oil Diffuser

Elevate your space with our elegant aroma diffuser, designed to bring a touch of nature and calm to your home or office. Perfect for large rooms, this diffuser combines powerful performance with a unique aesthetic.

Enhance Your Atmosphere

With our 360ml capacity diffuser, you can enjoy up to 48 hours of soothing aromaterapy. Simply add a few drops of the included 1.76 oz Hotel Collection essential oils and let the fragrant mist fill your space. Create a calming environment for relaxation, meditation, or bedtime routines.

Volcano & Flame Modes

Experience the magic of nature with two captivating modes: volcano and flame. The volcano mode delivers a gentle, misty spray that mimics the calming rhythm of a glacier, while the flame mode offers a dynamic, swirling effect like rising smoke. Colorful Lighting

Humidifying Function

More than just an aroma diffuser, this versatile device also works as a humidifier. Its spray system keeps the air moist for extended periods, helping to maintain a comfortable humidity level in your large room. Improve your breathing and overall well-being with fresh, crisp air.
