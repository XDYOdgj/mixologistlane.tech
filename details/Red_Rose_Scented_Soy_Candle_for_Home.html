KONNI Scented Candles for Home

Elevate your space with the soothing allure of our 9oz Soy Candles, infused with the delicate scent of red rose. Designed for both relaxation and ambiance, these candles offer a unique blend of beauty, aroma, and sustainability.

Enchanting Red Rose Aroma

Our scented candles are made with a handmade natural wax base and a lead-free cotton wick for a clean, safe burn. The premium rose essential oils deliver a fresh and pure fragrance that promotes relaxation, calms the mind, and enhances your mood with every flicker.

Long-Lasting & Eco-Friendly

With a generous 9oz (260ML) size, these candles burn for up to 35-40 hours, ensuring a lasting scent throughout your home. They produce minimal soot and no toxic fumes. After burning, the brown ceramic container can be reused, making it a sustainable and stylish addition to your decor.

Luxurious Home Decor

Choose KONNI for a sensory experience that enhances your living space. These candles are ideal for creating a serene environment and are perfect for those who value balance, tranquility, and a refreshed atmosphere for their daily moments of peace.

Perfect Gift for Every Occasion

Wrap up the perfect gift with our premium packaging, ideal for birthdays, Mother’s Day, Father’s Day, anniversaries, friend gatherings, or Valentine’s Day. KONNI Candles are an elegant and thoughtful way to show love, care, and appreciation with a lasting impression.

Burning Safety Tips

For an optimal and safe experience, always place your candle on a heat-resistant plate or coaster to prevent damage to wooden surfaces. Our candles are crafted with your well-being in mind, ensuring a satisfying experience from the first light to the last.
