M&SENSE Citrus Berry Scented Candles

Experience the perfect blend of relaxation and style with M&SENSE's Citrus Berry Scented Candles. These large 3-wick soy tin candles are crafted with a sustainable formula, made from 100% natural, vegan wax and wrapped in a beautifully designed tin jar that doubles as a decorative piece and functional storage.

Why Choose M&SENSE?

Thoughtfully designed to enhance your living space, these sustainable candles offer a rich, long-lasting fragrance that fills your room with a calming and refreshing aroma. The high-quality blend of America-grown soy and vegetable wax ensures a clean burn with minimal soot, making them ideal for creating a cozy and inviting ambiance in any room.

Each candle features 3 self-trimming cotton wicks, which work together to deliver a stronger scent throw, perfect for larger areas. With a burning time of over 40 hours, you can enjoy the soothing scent of citrus and berries throughout the day or evening. The 12oz net weight makes it a substantial and versatile addition to your candle collection.

Aesthetic & Functional Design

The tin candles combine a modern aesthetic with a touch of vintage elegance. Adorned with exquisite floral patterns, each piece is not only a functional candle but also a beautiful home accent. When the candle has burned out, the jar can be repurposed to store your favorite jewelry or small decor items, adding to the product's eco-friendly appeal.

Perfect Gift for Everyone

Whether you're looking for a gift for women, men, or anyone who appreciates the art of aromatherapy, these CitrusBerry candles are a thoughtful and stylish choice. They make for an excellent gift that brings comfort, beauty, and a touch of luxury to any home or personal space. Explore various scents to find the one that fits your lifestyle or that of the person you love.
