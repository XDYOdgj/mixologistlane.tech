WoodWick Renew Large Candle

Indulge in the calming power of natural aromatherapy with the WoodWick Renew Large Candle. Infused with the rich, inviting scents of black currant and rose, this candle offers a soothing experience that transforms your space into a peaceful sanctuary.

Eco-Conscious Craftsmanship

Our candles are made from plant-based soy wax blends and coconut wax, ensuring a clean burn and long-lasting fragrance. Free from harsh dyes and unnecessary additives, they are a healthier choice for your home and the environment.

Sustainable Design

Each candle is housed in an amber glass vessel crafted using 55% post-consumer recycled glass, making it both beautiful and responsible. The cork lid is sourced from responsibly harvested trees, adding to the candle's eco-friendly appeal.

Pure and Natural Aromas

Experience the purity of aromatherapy with fragrances developed from bio-based ingredients, upcycled materials, and essential oils. This blend is designed to promote relaxation and well-being without synthetic chemicals.

Long-Lasting Burn Time

With a burn time of up to 75 hours, the WoodWick Renew Large Candle is perfect for creating a peaceful ambiance over an extended period. Enjoy a blend of style, sustainability, and scent in every flicker.
