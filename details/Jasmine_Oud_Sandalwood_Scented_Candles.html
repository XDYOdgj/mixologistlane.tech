Putart Jasmine, Oud & Sandalwood Scented Candles

Elevate your space with a blend of calming and exotic scents designed for relaxation and self-care. These natural soy wax candles are perfect for home fragrance, aromatherapy, and thoughtful gifting.

Luxurious Aromatherapy Experience

Our scented candles combine the soothing essence of jasmine, the deep warmth of oud, and the smooth, woody notes of sandalwood. With 15% high-concentration essential oils, each candle offers a rich yet balanced aroma that lingers beautifully and promotes relaxation, stress relief, and better sleep.

100% Natural & Safe for All

Handmade with pure soy wax and free from harmful chemicals like phthalates, parabens, and formaldehyde, these candles ensure a safe and healthy environment for your family. Ideal for homes with children, pets, or anyone seeking a toxin-free lifestyle.

Long-Lasting Burn with Clean Performance

Featuring lead-free cotton wicks, these candles burn evenly and last up to 50 hours without soot or smoke. Perfect for creating a serene ambiance in your living room, bedroom, office, or bathroom, whether you're winding down after a long day or preparing for an evening of relaxation.

Ideal for Gifting and Celebrations

Presented in elegant frosted white glass jars with golden lids and premium gift boxes, these candles are a perfect choice for any occasion. Whether it's Valentine's Day, Christmas, birthdays, or housewarming events, they make a meaningful and stylish gift for loved ones and colleagues alike.

Eco-Friendly and Reusable

Committed to sustainability, our candles are crafted from eco-friendly materials. After use, the glass jars can be cleaned and reused as planters, storage containers, or decorative pieces, helping you reduce waste and embrace a greener lifestyle.

Create a Cozy Atmosphere

Whether you're practicing yoga, meditating, taking a bath, or simply unwinding, these candles enhance your self-care routine with their calming and inviting aromas. They bring a touch of luxury and comfort to your daily life and special moments.
