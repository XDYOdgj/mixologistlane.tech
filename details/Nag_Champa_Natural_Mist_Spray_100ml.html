Nag Champa Natural Mist Spray by Aromafume

100 ml / 3.3oz – Pure, handcrafted, and infused with the essence of sacred flowers

Experience the calming power of nature with our Nag Champa Natural Mist Spray. Created using fresh Champa flower extracts harvested at dawn, this mist is gently blended with Sandalwood, Jasmine, and Geranium to offer a rich, woody, and floral aroma that lingers beautifully in your space or on your fabrics.

Whether you're seeking peace for meditation, creating a sacred ambiance for rituals, or simply wanting to infuse your environment with tranquility, this mist is your perfect companion. Ideal for use during yoga, reading, self-reflection, and moments of purification. Its subtle yet powerful sillage ensures a peaceful presence in your living space, car, or gym bag.

Our mist is completely non-alcoholic, non-toxic, and vegan, making it safe for daily use and kind to the planet. Sustainably sourced and ethically harvested, every spray is a mindful act. You can breathe easy knowing your aromatherapy is free from harmful synthetics and cruelty-free in every sense.

Perfect for

  • Meditation and spiritual practices
  • Yoga sessions and self-care routines
  • Reading, relaxation, and mindfulness
  • Purification of spaces and fabrics

How to Use

Shake the bottle gently and spray a few times over your fabric, room, or surfaces. Let the delicate blend of scents embrace your space, or give yourself a spritz to enjoy a moment of calm and focus. Take a deep breath, and let the magic of Nag Champa awaken your senses.

Our Commitment

At Aromafume, we believe in creating products that not only enhance your well-being but also honor the earth. Our Nag Champa Mist is made with love, care, and ethical practices, ensuring a clean and uplifting experience for you and your surroundings.
