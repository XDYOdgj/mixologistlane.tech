Candle-Lite Scented Candles - Evening Fireside Glow, 18 oz Jar, Aromatherapy Candle

Proudly poured in the USA, these beautifully crafted single-wick candles bring warmth and serenity to any space. With an impressive 110 hours of burn time, they're perfect for those who value both quality and ambiance.

CREATE A REFRESHING ATMOSPHERE

Immerse yourself in the soothing aromas of red cedar, earthy patchouli, and sandalwood, combined with golden amber, fresh balsam, vanilla, and a warm hint of cinnamon. This blend creates a woodsy, comforting aroma that transforms your room into a peaceful retreat.

SPARKLING FRAGRANCE NOTES

Experience a layered scent with top notes of camphor and cinnamon, middle notes of cedar, patchouli, and sandalwood, and a rich base of golden amber, balsam, and vanilla. The fragrance is balanced, inviting, and perfect for relaxation.

LONG-LASTING, SUSTAINABLE DESIGN

Our candles are made from clean, natural ingredients with a single-wick design that ensures a steady burn for up to 110 hours. Free from parabens, sulfates, phthalates, and animal testing, they're a responsible choice for eco-conscious consumers.

IDEAL FOR GIFTING OR RELAXING

Whether you're looking to relax after a long day, create a cozy atmosphere for movie night, or find the perfect gift for someone special, this 18 oz jar candle is the ideal choice. Its elegant design and comforting aroma make it a favorite for both men and women.

Indulge in the art of aromatherapy with Candle-Lite. Each candle is a sensory experience, designed to bring comfort, style, and warmth to your everyday life.
