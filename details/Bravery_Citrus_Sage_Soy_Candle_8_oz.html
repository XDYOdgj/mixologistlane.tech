YES YOU CANDLE - Bravery, Citrus & Sage Soy Candle

Discover your true strength with this bold, inspirationscented candle crafted in the USA. Perfect for those who seek empowerment and a touch of natural fragrance in their daily lives.

Inspiration & Courage

This candle is more than a fragrance — it's a reminder to embrace your courage and uncover the best parts of yourself. When you're inspired to take bold steps, you pave the way for a great story to unfold.
