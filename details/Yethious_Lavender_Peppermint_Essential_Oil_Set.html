yethious Lavender & Peppermint Essential Oil Set

Explore the calming power of nature with this exquisite 2-pack essential oil set from yethious. Each 10ml bottle is filled with pure lavender and peppermint oils, free from any additives or fillers, ensuring you get the highest quality aromatherapy experience.

Lavender essential oil, extracted from the delicate lavender flowers, brings a soothing, sweet, and romantic ambiance to any space. Peppermint essential oil, derived from fresh peppermint leaves, offers a refreshing and invigorating scent that can elevate your mood and energy levels.

At yethious, we are committed to delivering only the best. Every batch of our aromatherapy oils is rigorously tested to maintain purity and potency, giving you the confidence to use them for relaxation, wellness, or creative projects.

This versatile essential oil set is perfect for use in diffusers, homemade perfumes, bath products, candles, and more. Transform your home, office, or car into a tranquil retreat with the natural aromas of lavender and peppermint, designed to enhance your daily life and promote a sense of calm.
