Edens Garden Good Night Essential Oil Blend

Experience the ultimate in calming aromas with our 10 ml (0.33 fl oz) Good Night Essential Oil Blend. Designed for rest and rejuvenation, this premium, undiluted blend is perfect for diffusing, candle use, or adding to your evening routine.

Tranquil Nighttime Harmony

Infuse your space with a serene, soothing atmosphere using our Good Night Essential Oil Blend. Blended with the gentle tones of Lavender, Sweet Marjoram, Chamomile, Bergamot, Ylang Ylang, Sandalwood, Key Lime, Vanilla CO2, and Roman Chamomile, this mix creates a calming scent ideal for unwinding after a busy day.

Wellness Benefits

More than just an aromatic indulgence, this blend is crafted to support your evening wellness rituals. It offers a peaceful backdrop for your bedtime routine, promoting relaxation, clarity, and a restful night. Ideal for stress relief and self-care.

100% Pure Essential Oils

  • Lavender - Bulgarian (Lavandula angustifolia)
  • Sweet Marjoram - Origanum majorana
  • Chamomile - Roman (Anthemis nobilis)
  • Bergamot - Citrus bergamia
  • Ylang Ylang - Cananga odorata
  • Sandalwood - Hawaiian (Santalum paniculatum)
  • Key Lime - Citrus aurantifolia var. swingle
  • Vanilla CO2 - Vanilla planifolia

Versatile Usage

Enjoy the versatility of our Good Night Essential Oil Blend. Diffuse it in your bedroom to promote relaxation. Mix with a carrier oil for a soothing massage. Use a few drops on a cotton ball and place under your pillow for a calming scent as you sleep.

Premium & Affordable

At Edens Garden, we bring you high-grade essential oils at great prices. Our commitment to quality ensures that every product is crafted to deliver value and performance without breaking the bank. Perfect for those who want the best in aromatherapy without the premium cost.

Woman-Owned & Family-Operated

Since 2009, Edens Garden has been a woman-owned, family-operated business. We personally source and blend each oil to maintain our high standards. With a wide selection of natural, eco-friendly home and body products, we've earned our place as a trusted name in aromatherapy.

Meet Our Licensed Aromatherapists

Our team of licensed aromatherapists is dedicated to your wellness journey. We offer expert guidance and support, ensuring that every blend you choose aligns with your goals and lifestyle. Discover the healing power of essential oils with us.
