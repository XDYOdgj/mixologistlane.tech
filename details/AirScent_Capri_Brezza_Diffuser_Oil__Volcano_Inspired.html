AirScent Capri Brezza Diffuser Oil

Experience the vibrant and invigorating aroma of our Volcano-Inspired Premium Fragrance Oil No. 1100. Perfect for use in ultrasonic and waterless diffusers, this 10 mL glass bottle features a dropper for precise application. Crafted with care, it's ideal for creative DIY projects like candle making and soap making.

Fragrant Notes

Fruity orange rind, uplifting grapefruit, and sweet, succulent pineapple open with a burst of freshness. This is followed by a warm blend of raspberry, mango, and a rich, sensual finish of sandalwood and erotic musk. The result is a balanced, long-lasting fragrance that lingers in every room.

Exact Duplication of a Beloved Blend

This diffuser oil is a perfect replica of the popular blue volcanic blend, capturing the essence of sugared citrus, exotic fruits, and creamy woody and musky notes. Whether you're aromatherapy enthusiast or looking to enhance your living space, this scent offers a unique and memorable experience.

High-Quality, Clean Ingredients

As a trusted fragrance manufacturer, we ensure our oils are made with the highest concentration of natural ingredients. They're free from synthetic colors, parabens, and phthalates, and do not contain any carrier oils. This guarantees a pure and concentrated aroma that's safe for everyday use.

Versatile Use in Diffusers

Our essential oils are designed to work seamlessly with a variety of diffusers, including ultrasonic diffusers, water-based diffusers, reed diffusers, and cold air diffusers. Simply add a few drops to your favorite diffuser and enjoy the calming and uplifting effects of this powerful blend.

Fragrance for Crafting

Crafters will love the versatility of this oil, which can be incorporated into a wide array of DIY projects. From candle making to soap making and more, it's an excellent choice for those who want to create custom aromas and infuse their handmade products with a unique touch.
