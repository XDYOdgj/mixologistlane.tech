INXDOLHOM 500ml Essential Oil Diffuser with 10 Scented Essential Oils

Transform your space into a sanctuary of calm and freshness with this stylish and functional aromatherapy diffuser. Designed with a beautiful wood grain finish, it adds a natural touch to any room while providing the benefits of essential oils and humidification.

Enhance Your Environment with Aromatherapy and Humidification

This diffuser isn’t just for aromatherapy—it also acts as a humidifier, helping maintain healthy air quality in your home. Perfect for neutralizing pet odors, smoke smells, or other unwanted scents, it keeps your living space fresh and comfortable, especially during dry seasons and in dusty environments.

Smart Features for Customized Use

With a remote control, 7 color light options, and 4 timer settings, you can easily adjust the ambiance of your room. The diffuser also has an auto shut-off function when the water level is low, ensuring safety and convenience. Choose between mist modes for a gentle or stronger output, ideal for large rooms, offices, or bedrooms.

Long-Lasting Performance

Thanks to its 500ml water tank, this diffuser can run for 10 to 15 hours on the low mist setting. It delivers 50 to 70ml of mist per hour, making it perfect for creating a cool and fragrant atmosphere wherever you need it.

Safe for All Ages

Constructed from premium PP material, the same used in baby bottles, this diffuser provides a soothing stream of cool, room-temperature mist. No heat is involved, making it safe for use around babies, pregnant women, and even pets.
