Hotel Luxury Essential Oil Gift Set (3x20mL)

Indulge your senses with this exquisite collection of three premium essential oils, designed to bring sophistication and calm to your space. Perfect for creating a luxurious atmosphere, whether at home, in the car, at the hotel, or in the office.

Sophisticated Scent Selection

This set features three elegantly crafted essential oils: My Way, Bergamot 22, and Oud Wood. Each offers a unique and lasting fragrance experience, ideal for diffusers, or for personal use in aromatherapy rituals. The scents are carefully blended to evoke a sense of refinement and relaxation, making them perfect for those who appreciate quality and style.

Enhanced Diffusion Experience

Designed for cold air diffusers, these oils are waterless and highly concentrated, ensuring your aromatherapy remains pure and potent. Their formulation allows them to work seamlessly with ultrasonic and nebulizing diffusers, delivering a deep and long-lasting fragrance without any dilution.

Fragrance Notes

My Way: Opens with a fresh zesty note of lemon, transitions into the warm aroma of sandalwood, and ends with a soft, powdery sweetness of iris. A perfect balance of natural and elegant tones.
Bergamot 22: Combines citrus and floral notes with bergamot, magnolia, and koa wood, offering a vibrant and uplifting aroma ideal for daily use.
Oud Wood: A rich, woody blend featuring oud, cedarwood, and vanilla. Perfect for creating a warm and grounding atmosphere, ideal for meditation or relaxation.

Stylish & Portable Packaging

Each oil is housed in a sleek, leak-proof bottle, making this gift set an ideal choice for travel, gifting, or personal use. Whether you're looking to enhance your living space or gift a touch of luxury to someone special, this set offers convenience and elegance in equal measure.

Easy to Use & Versatile

These essential oils are simple to apply and adaptable to different settings. Whether you use them in reed diffusers, oil diffusers, or even blend them with unscented candles, they provide a hassle-free way to transform any environment into a serene and luxurious experience.

Perfect for All Occasions

With its elegant design and premium quality, this essential oil set is a thoughtful and stylish gift. It's perfect for birthdays, holidays, housewarmings, or weddings, offering a refined touch that speaks to both sophistication and personal care.
