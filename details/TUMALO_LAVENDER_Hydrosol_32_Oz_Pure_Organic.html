What is Lavender Hydrosol?

Tumalo Lavender's hydrosol is crafted through the gentle steam distillation of fresh lavender flowers and stems. Unlike essential oils, which capture the concentrated essence of the plant, our hydrosol includes more of the natural components, resulting in a milder, earthy aroma that is both soothing and refreshing. This process ensures a pure, organic product that is gentle on your skin and safe for daily use.

Uses of Our Hydrosol

Whether you're looking to enhance your skincare routine, create a calming ambiance in your home, or simply enjoy the therapeutic benefits of lavender, our hydrosol is the perfect addition. It can be used as a facial toner, body mist, room spray, or linen spray. Its versatility makes it an essential part of any aromatherapy collection or personal care kit.
