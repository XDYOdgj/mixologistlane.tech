Eternal Essence Oils Housekeeping Set of 6 - Premium Fragrance Oils

Discover the art of scent with our Housekeeping Set of 6 Premium Fragrance Oils. Designed for use in candles, soaps, air fresheners, and more, these scents bring a refreshing and inviting aroma to your home and self-care routines.

Fruity & Summer Breeze Scents

Each oil is formulated to capture the essence of nature, offering a blend of clean cotton, lemon blossom, lemon grass, sweet pea, ocean breeze, and mountain rain. These seasonal fragrances are perfect for creating a cozy, inviting atmosphere or gifting to loved ones.

Long-Lasting & Rich Aroma

With notes of pine, gardenia, and cedar wood, these oils deliver a captivating and memorable fragrance. The middle and base notes offer depth and warmth, ideal for both aromatherapy and home use.

Enhance Your Self-Care Rituals

Bring a personal touch to your daily routines by adding these oils to your diffuser, body scrubs, massage oils, or even your hair care regimen. Each oil is created for external use, allowing you to enjoy its benefits safely and naturally.

Premium & Stylish Packaging

Each 10 ml bottle is housed in an elegant amber glass container and features a tamperproof black polypropylene cap with a dropper insert. This makes it easy to dispense just the right amount of oil for your needs.

At Eternal Essence Oils, we are committed to offering high-quality, naturally inspired scents that enhance your well-being and elevate your lifestyle. Our essential oil sets are designed for both functionality and beauty, making them the perfect addition to your home or self-care collection.
