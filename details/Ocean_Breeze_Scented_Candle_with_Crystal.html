Ocean Breeze Scented Candle with Crystal Inside

A beautifully crafted aromatherapy candle, infused with natural healing properties and designed to provide a calming, uplifting atmosphere.

Experience the Aroma of the Sea

Immerse yourself in the refreshing scent of the ocean with AROMOUNT's Ocean Breeze Scented Candle. This candle is a sensory journey that combines the crisp, clean top notes of bergamot, ozonic, and galbanum with the oceanic middle notes of marine and cyclamen, and the grounding base notes of seaweed, cedarwood, and mossy. The blend is designed to create a soothing ambiance and uplift your mood with every burn.

Long-Lasting & Eco-Friendly Design

Indulge in a 10oz candle with an impressive 55-60 hour burn time. Made from pure soy wax and lead-free cotton wick, it ensures a clean, safe, and long-lasting experience. Free from toxins, dyes, and paraffin, this candle is an ideal choice for those who value sustainability and quality.

Handcrafted with Love

Every AROMOUNT candle is made with care, featuring handpicked crystals and decorative elements. Each candle includes genuine aquamarine stones, sea stars, and conches, creating a visually captivating piece. Please note that the crystals are naturally occurring and may differ slightly in color, shape, or size, making each candle truly unique.

Healing Properties of Aquamarine

Aquamarine is known for its calming and soothing energy, helping to inspire truth, trust, and emotional release. It was traditionally believed to be the treasure of mermaids and was often carried by sailors as a symbol of good luck, courage, and protection. This candle harnesses the spiritual benefits of aquamarine, making it a powerful tool for relaxation and well-being.

Perfect Spiritual Gift

Wrap up this exquisite candle in a beautifully designed gift box and present it to loved ones or treat yourself. The luxurious matte finish glass jar and gold foiled logo add an elegant touch, while the inclusion of healing gemstones and sea-inspired decor make it a meaningful, spiritually resonant gift for any occasion.

Enhance your space, your mood, and your spiritual practice with AROMOUNT's Ocean Breeze Scented Candle. A beautiful blend of nature, style, and healing.
