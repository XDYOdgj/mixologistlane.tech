Holamay Fruit Essential Oils Set 30ml x 10 - Coconut, Strawberry, Mango, Pineapple & More

Transform your home into a cozy, fruity retreat with the Holamay Fruit Essential Oils Set. This collection features 10 premium 30ml bottles of pure, high-concentration natural oils, each infused with the vibrant scent of real fruits like coconut, strawberry, mango, pineapple, and more. A perfect blend of aroma and versatility, these oils can be used for diffusers, candle making, soap crafting, and even body care products.

High Capacity & Exceptional Quality
Each oil comes in a large 30ml size, offering long-lasting use and true-to-nature fragrance. Our oils are crafted with care and rigor, ensuring a rich and consistent scent for every application.

Premium & Safe for Every Use
Formulated to meet strict IFRA standards, Holamay oils are safe to use for aromatherapy, candles, and personal care. No fillers, additives, or paraben are included, making them ideal for sensitive skin and eco-conscious creators.

Easy to Use & Versatile
Perfect for both DIY enthusiasts and those looking to enhance their space, this set is designed for effortless blending. Whether you're making candles, soap, lotions, or using the oils in diffusers, the high flash point ensures safety and excellent performance.

Refresh Your Environment Instantly
Bring a burst of freshness into your home with a few drops of these oils in your diffuser or humidifier. The crisp, tropical scents are long-lasting and perfect for creating a relaxing ambiance, especially in the cooler months.

Ideal for Gift Giving & Seasonal Touches
Looking for a thoughtful gift? This essential oil set is a delightful choice for friends, family, or coworkers. It adds a unique and aromatic touch to any occasion, making it a perfect addition to Thanksgiving, Christmas, or any festive celebration.
