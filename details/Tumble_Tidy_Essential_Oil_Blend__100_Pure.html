Tumble & Tidy Essential Oil Blend by Revive Essential Oils

ReviveEO

Discover the natural way to refresh your laundry with the Tumble & Tidy Essential Oil Blend. This powerful combination of Lavender, Lime, Rosemary, Tea Tree, Bergamot, Ylang Ylang, Geranium, Sweet Orange, Litsea, and Jasmine oils is designed to replace harsh chemicals and deliver a fresh, clean scent without the need for synthetic fragrances.

Perfect for use in your dryer, this essential oil blend is especially useful when you add it to unscented dryer sheets or wool dryer balls. Simply toss it in with your wet clothes and enjoy a naturally fragrant, clean laundry every time. It's also great for diffusers, humidifiers, massage, and natural skincare and hair care routines.

The Tumble & Tidy essential oil blend offers more than just a pleasant aroma. It can help promote calm feelings, support digestion, ease stress, and even relieve tension in the head and neck area. Its versatility makes it an excellent addition to your wellness and home care routine.

At ReviveEO, we believe in pure, plant-based ingredients that do more than just smell good. Our essential oils are 100% natural, extracted from plants around the world. We source them from the regions where each kind of plant or tree thrives best, ensuring quality and potency in every drop.

We are proud to offer direct-to-consumer essential oils online. By selling through the internet, we eliminate the need for middlemen and distributors, giving you the same high-quality oils at a better price. Our commitment is to provide pure, therapeutic grade oils that serve you and your family with natural care and support.
