Muse Apothecary Pillow Ritual - Aromatic, Calming and Relaxing Pillow Mist, Linen and Fabric Spray

Infused with Natural Aromatherapy Essential Oils - 8 oz, Fresh Snow, 2 Pack

Transform your sleep space with the Muse Apothecary Pillow Ritual Mist. This luxurious aromatherapy blend is designed to bring calm and relaxation to your daily life. Simply spritz it onto your pillows, bedding, or any surface to create a soothing sensory experience that promotes peaceful rest.

With a refreshing and clean formula, this mist is perfect for those who seek a natural way to enhance their well-being. It blends therapeutic aromas that nourish both your mind and body, helping you unwind after a long day and drift into deeper, more restorative sleep.

A Winter Escape in Every Spritz

The Fresh Snow scent is a calming ode to a peaceful winter retreat. Infused with winter lily, jasmine, cedarwood, and fresh sea salt, it evokes the quiet joy of a snowy day. This mist not only refreshes your linens but also creates a spa-like atmosphere in your home.

Perfect for holidays, birthdays, or as a thoughtful gift, this 2-pack of 8-ounce pillow mist is a gift that keeps on giving. Whether you're treating yourself or a loved one, the Muse Apothecary Pillow Ritual offers a natural, luxurious way to embrace relaxation and inner peace.
