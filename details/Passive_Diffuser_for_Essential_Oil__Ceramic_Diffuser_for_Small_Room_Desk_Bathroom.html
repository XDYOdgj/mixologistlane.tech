Oumeiben Passive Diffuser for Essential Oil - Ceramic Diffuser, Non-Electric, Mini Room & Desk Decorative

Elevate your space with the Oumeiben passive diffuser, a beautiful and compact aromatherapy solution designed for small areas like your desk, bathroom, or bedroom. This non-electric diffuser is perfect for creating a calming environment without the need for power or batteries.

Natural Aroma Diffusion

Our diffuser uses a unique design that allows essential oils to slowly diffuse through the petals. Simply place your favorite oil on the spread stone and let it blend with the ceramic flower, releasing a subtle and soothing scent into your surroundings.

Premium & Safe Materials

Constructed with high-quality ceramic and made from medium temperature soil, the Oumeiben diffuser is safe to use and completely eco-friendly. The ceramic base ensures a secure fit and prevents any essential oil leakage onto your desk or surface.

Simple & Effortless Use

Use the passive diffuser by adding 7 to 10 drops of essential oil to the spread stone. It can be left to work for up to 1 to 2 weeks, making it ideal for daily use in small spaces. The gentle airflow enhances the natural diffusion of the oil, creating a more immersive scent experience.

Elegant & Meaningful Gift

Packed with unique design and a thoughtful concept, the Oumeiben diffuser is an elegant birthday, anniversary, or personal gift for women, girls, parents, and friends. It adds a delightful touch of aromatherapy to your home or office, helping you relax, unwind, and enjoy better sleep.
