Pumpkin Spice Candles for Home Scented

Experience the warmth of fall with our luxury pumpkin soy wax candles. Infused with 15% high-concentration premium essential oils, these candles release a rich, inviting aroma that's both comforting and never overpowering. Perfect for creating a cozy, calming atmosphere at home, they help you unwind, relax, and enjoy better sleep.

Premium & Handcrafted Quality

Made from 100% pure, natural soy wax, our candles are safe, non-toxic, and free from harmful chemicals like phthalates, parabens, and formaldehyde. Ideal for families with children and pets, they offer a healthier and more eco-conscious alternative to traditional paraffin candles. Plus, they help purify the air and leave your space smelling fresh and welcoming.

Long Lasting & Clean Burn

Each candle is designed with lead-free cotton wicks for a stable, even burn that lasts up to 50 hours. The smooth, clean burn ensures no soot or black smoke, providing a delightful, enduring fragrance for your living room, bedroom, or workspace. Whether you're winding down after work or preparing for a yoga session, these candles deliver a soothing aromatic experience.

Perfect Gifts for All Occasions

Wrap up your gift with our elegant, luxurious orange jar candles. These candle gifts are thoughtfully packaged in stylish boxes, making them ideal for holidays like Halloween, Christmas, and Thanksgiving, or special moments such as birthdays, anniversaries, Mother's Day, and more. A unique and meaningful gift for women, men, couples, or colleagues that adds a touch of warmth and style to any occasion.

Eco-Friendly & Sustainable Design

We're committed to sustainability with every candle. After burning, the glass jars can be repurposed as functional storage containers, small planters, or decorative accents. Our eco-friendly approach ensures you receive a high-quality product while also supporting a healthier planet. Choose Putart for a mindful, luxurious, and sustainable home fragrance experience.
