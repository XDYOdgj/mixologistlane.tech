SEVEYEE 500ml Aromatherapy Diffuser - White

Transform your space with the SEVEYEE 500ml Aromatherapy Diffuser. Designed for large rooms, this diffuser blends sleek aesthetics with advanced technology to bring you the perfect blend of relaxation, freshness, and ambiance.

Create a Romantic Atmosphere

Whether it's Valentine's Day, an anniversary, or any special occasion, this diffuser adds a floral touch to your home. Its vibrant colors and soothing scents make it a perfect gift for your loved ones, helping to set a romantic mood, eliminate stale air, and create a cozy environment for intimacy.

All-Day Operation with Smart Auto Off

With a 500ml large water tank, the SEVEYEE diffuser can run up to 24 hours on normal mode or 20 hours on strong mist mode. Equipped with a 1-hour timer, it ensures convenient use throughout the day. The diffuser automatically turns off when the water level drops below the MAX line for added safety and peace of mind.

Clean, Fresh Air for Better Health

Experience the benefits of aromatherapy with this ultrasonic diffuser. Unlike traditional heating methods, it uses cool mist to enhance air quality and maintain optimal humidity, which is perfect for protecting your skin from dryness. The gentle, natural scents also help to reduce stress, uplift your mood, and improve your sleep quality.

7 Color Night Lights for a Personal Touch

Enhance your nighttime experience with the 7-color LED light options. Choose from calming blue, soothing green, warm yellow, or vibrant red to match your mood. The diffuser can cycle through all colors or stay fixed on your preferred one, making it a beautiful ambient light for your home and a comforting companion for your baby.

Easy Maintenance and User-Friendly Design

Its large top mouth allows for easy filling, cleaning, and refilling of essential oils. This design makes it one of the most convenient diffusers on the market, and the removable flower section adds to its practicality. Simply follow the instructions to avoid overfilling, which can clog the mist outlet and reduce performance.

BPA-Free and extremely quiet, with a noise level under 25dB, this diffuser blends safety, performance, and style for a truly immersive relaxation experience.
