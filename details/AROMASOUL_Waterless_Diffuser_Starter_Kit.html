AROMASOUL Waterless Scent Diffuser Starter Kit

Create a spa-like atmosphere in your home or office with our premium cordless, rechargeable diffuser. Designed for luxurious use, this set offers 1000 sq ft. fragrance coverage, includes 120ml essential oils, and features smart app control for customized aromatherapy experiences.

Perfect for hotels, living rooms, bedrooms, and more, this diffuser delivers a clean, residue-free mist using advanced cold air diffusion technology. No water required, no messy cleanup, only pure and long-lasting scents that transform your space into a calming retreat.

Luxurious Design & Purity

Featuring a sleek, durable metal body with an anodized finish, this diffuser is built to last while exuding elegance. The upgraded atomization system ensures essential oils are released in a fine, pure mist, providing a richer and more prolonged fragrance without any residue. The 120ml hotel-grade oils are crafted with natural ingredients and vegan formulas, free from harmful chemicals for a safe and soothing environment.

Unplugged Convenience

Enjoy the freedom of a cordless, rechargeable design that can run for up to 10 days on a single charge. Say goodbye to tangled cords and frequent charging. Use the intermittent or continuous mode to match your mood, with the quiet operation of the all-metal body ensuring a peaceful experience in any setting.

Intuitive Smart App Control

Download the AROMA PRO app to effortlessly control your diffuser. Customize fragrance intensity, set personalized schedules, and manage operating time to optimize your essential oil use. Whether you want a calm, invigorating, or relaxing scent, this diffuser lets you create the perfect ambiance with just a few taps on your phone.

Complete Starter Kit

Includes everything you need to start your aromatherapy journey: one waterless diffuser, one 120ml fragrance oil bottle, two refill bottles, a detailed instruction manual, after-sales service card, Bluetooth connection guide, a cleaning cloth, and a USB-C cable. The premium multi-layer packaging ensures your device arrives in perfect condition, protected by a high-density sponge lining.

Transform your environment with AROMASOUL. Experience the beauty of natural scents, the comfort of uninterrupted use, and the elegance of a modern, smart aromatherapy solution. Perfect for personal use, hospitality, or relaxation at home.
