MOLIMAO Magnolia Flower Diffuser Stone - Non-Electric, Handmade Aromatherapy Decor

Bring a touch of nature into your space with this beautifully crafted magnolia-shaped diffuser stone. Perfect for those who love the calming effects of aromatherapy without the need for electricity or batteries.

Durable & Handmade Design

This diffuser stone is made from high-quality gypsum and features a natural, wooden base, ensuring it is safe, pollution-free, and entirely handmade. Each piece is unique, adding a charming handmade touch to your home or workspace.

Simple & Effective Use

Simply add 5 to 8 drops of your favorite essential oil to the stone, and enjoy the gentle, continuous release of aroma for up to a week. It's ideal for small spaces or areas where you want to create a calming atmosphere.

Versatile & Stylish

Whether you're placing it on your desk, dressing table, bedside, or even in your car, this diffuser stone is both practical and aesthetically pleasing. It's a perfect decorative piece that complements any interior style.

Dimensions & Weight

Measuring 3.1 inches in length and width, with a height of 2 inches, this diffuser stone weighs approximately 150 grams. Keep in mind that due to its handmade nature, there may be slight variations in size and weight.

Care & Tips

For optimal use, avoid placing essential oils directly on the wooden base. Always use a small amount and allow the stone to diffuse naturally. This product is ideal for everyday use and enhances your overall well-being.

Why Choose MOLIMAO?

MOLIMAO combines elegance and functionality in every product. Our diffuser stones are designed with simplicity in mind, letting you enjoy a natural, soothing atmosphere without the hassle of complicated setups.
