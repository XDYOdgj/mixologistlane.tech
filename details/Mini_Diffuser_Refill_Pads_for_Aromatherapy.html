Maitys Mini Diffuser Refill Pads

Enhance your aromatherapy experience with Maitys' premium mini diffuser refill pads. Designed for both effectiveness and convenience, these round microfiber absorbing pads are perfect for your humidifier, oil diffuser, or aroma fan. Each one ensures a smooth and consistent release of your favorite essential oils, creating a soothing or invigorating atmosphere wherever you need it.

Key Features

  • Plentiful Supply: Receive 24 professional-grade diffuser refill pads, ideal for regular use and easy replacement. Perfect for maintaining your aromatherapy routine without frequent reordering.
