Capri Blue Signature Jar Candles

Luxury tropical scented candles made with long-lasting soy wax blend. Available in a sleek blue decorative glass jar, perfect for adding a touch of elegance to your living space.

8 oz Capri Blue Candle

Though small, this candle is packed with rich, tropical aromas that bring a refreshing and uplifting vibe to your room. Our Honeydew Crush candle blends notes of juicy honeydew nectar, zesty grapefruit, crisp green apple, and delicate peony blossom. The elegant blue glass jar adds a sophisticated look to your home decor, and the candle offers up to 40 hours of warm, inviting fragrance.

Luxury Soy Wax Blend

Experience the finest in aromatherapy with our soy wax blend that is both eco-friendly and long-lasting. The high-quality ingredients ensure a clean burn and an intense, lasting scent. Each candle is designed with a sturdy wick for even melting and a consistent, enjoyable burn time.

Candle Care Tips

To enjoy the fullest experience, allow the top layer of wax to melt completely before extinguishing. Trim the wick to 0.5 inches before the first burn and before lighting to ensure a smooth, long-lasting burn. Always keep the candle away from drafts and never leave it unattended. Repurpose our beautiful blue glass jars after use for storage, decor, or DIY projects.

About Capri Blue

Capri Blue is inspired by modern design and seasonal trends to create premium candles that blend style with quality. Our candles come in a variety of colored glass jars, each delivering a unique and luxurious scent experience. Whether you're looking for small candles for personal use or large candles to elevate your space, Capri Blue offers the perfect choice for any occasion.
