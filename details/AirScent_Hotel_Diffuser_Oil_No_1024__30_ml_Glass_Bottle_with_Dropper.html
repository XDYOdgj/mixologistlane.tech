AirScent Hotel Diffuser Oil Inspired by The JW Marriott's Oud Safir Scent - Fragrance Oil No. 1024

Elevate your space with the rich, captivating aroma of Oud Safir, a signature scent from The JW Marriott. This luxurious blend features a delicate balance of fresh green notes, followed by velvety plum, rose, Muguet, and soft amber, all wrapped in a warm embrace of raspberry, musk, vanilla, and a subtle tobacco-tinged rooty oud. It's a fragrance that brings sophistication and comfort into every room.

Crafted with precision, our concentrated essential oils are designed to deliver intense, long-lasting fragrance without the need for water. As a trusted fragrance manufacturer, we ensure our oils are free from synthetic colors, parabens, phthalates, and carrier oils, making them pure and safe for use in various diffuser types.

Whether you're using an ultrasonic diffuser, a waterless diffuser, reed diffuser, humidifier, or cold air diffuser, this oil adapts seamlessly to your needs. It's the perfect choice for those who appreciate the art of aromatherapy and want to infuse their environment with elegance and calm.

Beyond its use in personal diffusion, this oil also serves as a valuable resource for crafters. Bring your DIY projects to life with a high-quality aroma oil that can be used in candle making, soap making, and other creative endeavors. Explore the possibilities of crafting with this versatile and beautiful scent.

Each bottle features a Euro-style dropper cap for accurate and easy measurement, ensuring you get the perfect amount with no spills or waste. This makes it simple and efficient for both everyday use and seasonal creativity.
