Benatu Jasmine Lemongrass Essential Oil Blend Spray

A luxurious aromatherapy mist that brings the fresh fragrance of lemongrass and jasmine to your daily routines.

Purify and Refresh Your Space

This natural essential oil spray is crafted to purify and refresh your mind. Infused with the soothing essence of jasmine and the crisp aroma of lemongrass, it adds a calming, uplifting scent to your home, car, and personal items. Whether used in the bedroom, on your pillow, or in your linen, it creates a serene environment that enhances your well-being.

All-Natural Ingredients for a Healthier You

Containing only distilled water and pure essential oils, our aromatherapy spray is free from artificial fragrances and harmful additives. This ensures a gentle, non-irritating experience that can be safely used for extended periods without causing discomfort.

Safe and Effective for All Purposes

With a non-greasy, non-sticky formula, this essential oil mist is perfect for use on your face, hair, body, clothes, and even your car. Its versatility makes it a great addition to your daily self-care routine and home fragrance collection.

How to Use

For external use only. Shake well before each use and spray directly onto the desired area. It's ideal for personal fragrance, room misting, and aromatherapy applications.
