TALENT CANDLES - Pink Beach Sunset

Discover the calming power of aromatherapy with our Pink Beach Sunset scented candle. Crafted with natural soy wax and a premium, smokeless wick, this candle offers a pure and clean burn, making it perfect for unwinding in your home or bedroom.

Immerse yourself in the delicate blend of fruity and floral notes that naturally uplift your mood and create a peaceful ambiance. The soft woody undertones provide a grounding effect, helping you find inner calm and relaxation with every flicker of light.

Burns for Up to 40 Hours

Thanks to its quality construction, this candle delivers a long-lasting fragrance experience, ideal for those who enjoy a cozy, aromatic atmosphere throughout the day or night. Whether you're reading, meditating, or simply relaxing, it keeps the scent fresh and inviting.

Eco-Friendly & Sustainable

Our candle is made from 100% natural soy wax, sourced from renewable resources, and infused with organic ingredients to ensure a safe and environmentally conscious experience. It's a small but meaningful way to care for your space and the planet.

Exquisite Design with Built-In Lid

Each candle is enclosed in a beautifully finished bamboo jar with a secure lid, combining both function and style. The matte varnish adds a touch of sophistication while protecting the wax and helping to preserve the fragrance aroma for longer.

Handmade with care, our Pink Beach Sunset candle is a luxurious addition to any self-care routine. Light it up and let the soothing scent transport you to a serene coastal retreat, where stress melts away and calmness prevails.
