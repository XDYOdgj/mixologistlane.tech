Onaboviva Aromatherapy Diffuser

Elevate your space with this stylish and effective essential oil diffuser. Designed for both relaxation and ambiance, it combines modern aesthetics with advanced features to suit any setting.

Ultra-Silent Operation for Peaceful Relaxation

Experience calming aromatherapy without the noise. The ultrasonic technology creates a whisper-quiet mist, making it ideal for use in your bedroom, living room, office, or even spa-like spaces. Its minimalist design enhances any interior decor, blending seamlessly into your surroundings.

Safety First with BPA-Free Materials

Constructed with food-grade PP water tank and BPA-free materials, this diffuser ensures a safe and healthy environment for you and your family. The 120ml capacity allows for extended use without frequent refills.

Energy-Saving Auto Shut-Off

The diffuser automatically powers off when the water level is low, eliminating the need for constant monitoring and saving energy. This feature guarantees a worry-free experience, perfect for anyone who values convenience and sustainability.

Customizable Timer Options

Choose from 1-hour, 2-hour, 3-hour misting intervals or set it to continuous mode based on your needs. Whether you're winding down with a book, focusing during work, or meditating, this diffuser adapts to your lifestyle for ultimate relaxation.

Dual Mist Modes & Multi-Color Lighting

Enjoy two mist options: Strong or Gentle, to control the intensity of the aromatherapy. Pair it with three adjustable lighting effects to create the perfect atmosphere—whether for unwinding, staying focused, or adding a romantic touch to your space.

The warm ambient light is gentle on the eyes and enhances the overall sense of calm. It's the perfect addition to your bedroom, office, or living room, helping you relax and improve your sleep quality.

Easy to Use with Included Essentials

Designed for simplicity, this diffuser comes with everything you need to start enjoying its benefits: a durable ceramic diffuser, a USB cable for easy charging, and a detailed manual. Experience stress relief and a more peaceful living environment with our 365-day warranty for complete peace of mind.
