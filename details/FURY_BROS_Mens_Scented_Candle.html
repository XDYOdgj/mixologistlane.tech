FURY BROS. Scented Candles for Men | Supra 56 | Sandalwood, Patchouli, and Cedar

Crafted for the bold and the curious, this candle is a tribute to the spirit of adventure and rugged American style. With a mysterious, earthy aroma, it brings a touch of classic masculinity to any room.

Fragrance: Supra 56 - Sandalwood, Patchouli, and Cedar

Supra 56 is more than just a scent—it’s a journey. Inspired by the wisdom of seasoned explorers, its rich blend of sandalwood, patchouli, and cedar creates a signature aroma that’s both manly and timeless. After 55 meticulous trials, we perfected this fragrant experience to evoke the essence of the open road and the unbroken spirit of freedom.

Candle for Men

Designed with the man in mind, our candles are the perfect home gift for your dad or any man who appreciates authenticity. Whether it's for the man-cave or a thoughtful present, Fury Bros. candles are the ultimate expression of rugged elegance. Each is hand-poured in the USA, made with all-natural soy wax and crafted to stand the test of time.

High-Quality Ingredients

Our candles are vegan-friendly, nontoxic, and paraben-free, offering a pure and safe experience for your home. Made with the finest natural soy wax and a single cotton wick, they burn cleanly and evenly. The blend of natural oils gives your space a warm, inviting feel without any harsh chemicals.

Long Burn Time

With a generous 12.5 oz size, each Supra 56 candle burns for 70 to 80 hours, making it a great choice for long-lasting fragrance. To ensure optimal burn performance, we recommend trimming the wick before each use. This way, you get the most out of every candle, enjoying its aroma for weeks on end.

Vintage-Inspired Design

Featuring a retro tin design, Supra 56 is a tribute to classic American aesthetics. Each tin is proudly produced in the USA with a thick, high-quality vinyl label that adds a nostalgic touch to your interior. The design reflects the legacy of American motor oil cans, creating a bold and authentic statement in any space.

Our Story

Fury Bros. was born from the heart of two brothers, Jeremy and Stevie Fury, in the gritty streets of Hell's Kitchen. Inspired by their love for classic Americana, they founded the brand in 2015, bringing together the spirit of freedom, ruggedness, and adventure. Every candle is a piece of their story, crafted to embody the essence of a man who knows no limits.
